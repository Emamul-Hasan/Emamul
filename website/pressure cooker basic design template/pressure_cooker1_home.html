About Pressure Cooker

The media students of CIT are creating a Master Chef style cooking show called Pressure Cooker which will star the teachers and students of the culinary faculty of CIT. Two teams containing a culinary teacher and two students will go head to head each week to be crowned winner by a team of three judges. Episodes 1, 2 ,3, and 4 each have a different theme; breakfast, bar food, vegetarian, and fine dining respectively.
